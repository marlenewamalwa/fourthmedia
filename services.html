Broadcast
& Live Transmission

  • Multi-Camera Live Streaming Capture every angle with precision-orchestrated multi-camera setups. . From sports events and concerts to corporate keynotes, we manage shading, tally lights, audio mixing, and wireless comms—deploying rapidly for one-off shoots or ongoing series, with embedded encoders for platforms like YouTube, Zoom, or custom CDNs.
  • Real-Time Signal Distribution for Global Audiences — Reach viewers anywhere without interruptions. We distribute pristine signals via bonded cellular (e.g., TVU, LiveU), satellite uplinks (Intelsat, Eutelsat), and internet SRT/RTMP protocols, ensuring low-latency delivery to international broadcasters, OTT services, and social feeds. Redundant paths, automated failover, and 24/7 monitoring guarantee 99.99% uptime, scaling from local Kenyan audiences to global reach with multi-language commentary options.
  • Virtual and Hybrid Event Technical Direction Blend physical and digital worlds effortlessly. Our technical directors oversee LED walls, AR overlays, NDI/IP workflows, and audience interaction tools (e.g., Slido, Hopin integrations) for hybrid conferences, e-sports, or virtual productions. We provide end-to-end direction—from pre-vis planning and rehearsal coordination to live switching and post-event archiving—ensuring immersive, glitch-free experiences that engage remote and on-site participants alike.
